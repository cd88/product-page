The Mulchmate

A reusable garden tool that keeps yard waste bags open while they're being filled

Step 1
Insert the Mulchmate diagonally downward into a Lawn Bag
Step 2
Lift and guide the Mulchmate corners until snug against the bag corners
Step 3
Ready to use! The bag will stand & remain open while you fill it
